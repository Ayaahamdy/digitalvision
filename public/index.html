VALUE ADDED SERVICES (VAS)

Value-added services (VAS) is a popular telecommunications term for non-core services, or in short, all services beyond standard voice calls or other bearer only type services.

IVR SOLUTIONS OVERVIEW :

Historically, IVR solutions have used per-recorded voice prompts and menus to present information and options to callers, and touch-tone telephone keypad entry to gather responses. Modern IVR solutions also enable input and responses to be gathered via spoken words with voice recognition. IVR solutions enable users to retrieve information including bank balances, flight schedules, product details, order status, movie show times, and more from any telephone.
